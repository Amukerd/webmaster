Green Energy Initiatives for Homeowners

Introduction

Green energy initiatives are important to reduce the negative effects of fossil fuels on the environment. This website provides information for homeowners on green and clean energy home solutions and other initiatives.

Renewable Energy Programs

There are many renewable energy programs promoted and listed by the Environmental Protection Agency. These programs provide incentives for homeowners to include more green and clean energy solutions in their homes.

Cost and Tax Rebates

Learn about the costs associated with green energy solutions and find information on available tax rebates that can help reduce your expenses.

Explore best practices for incorporating clean and green energy solutions into your home and daily life to reduce your carbon footprint.
