Green energy, What is it? Green energy is using Clean energy, energy that doesn't use coal, oil, or other harmful fossil fuels that will harm our environment. 
Green energy comes in many different forms solar, wind, Biogas(landfill, gas), Geothermal, Biomass, Low-impact hydroelectricity, and Emerging Technologies. --Intro
